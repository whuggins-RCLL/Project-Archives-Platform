Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it privately by email:

whuggins@law.stanford.edu

Please include as much detail as possible to help us validate and address the issue quickly, such as:

A clear description of the issue

Steps to reproduce

Affected version(s)

Potential impact

Any proof-of-concept code, logs, or screenshots (if safe to share)

What to Expect
Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 5 business days.

Initial assessment: We will provide an initial triage/update within 10 business days.

Ongoing updates: We will share periodic status updates while investigating and remediating.

Resolution: If confirmed, we will work on a fix and coordinate disclosure timing with you when possible.

Disclosure Guidance
Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and release a fix.

Scope and Handling
Reports are reviewed on a best-effort basis.

If a report is accepted, we will prioritize remediation based on severity and affected versions.

If a report is declined (e.g., not reproducible, out of scope, or not a security issue), we will provide a brief explanation.

Thank you for helping keep this project and its users safe.
